First steps in the use of the management system

To take advantage of the use of this tool, there are a few steps to follow:

1. Create items and its attributes.

To create an item, go to the "Items" view and click the "Create new item" button. Enter the item's name, cost center, format (in numbers), unit of measurement and the format value. Then, click on "Save new item".

Make sure to add all items required for a product that you will create later. Once you have created them, they will be saved in the inventory. You can view the inventory at any time by going to the "Inventory" view.

2. Create a category of product and then create a new product that correspond to its category.

To create a category, go to the "Products" view and click the "Create product category" button. You only need to enter the category's name and save it.

Once you have created the category, you can create new products that belong to that category. To do this, go to the "Products" view and click the "New product" button. Enter the product's name, a category that you have previously created, an initial sell value and the number of components that will have.

3. Add a new supplier and then make a purchase of products.

To add a new supplier, go to the "Purchases" view and click the "New supplier" button. Enter the supplier's name and status.

Once you have added a supplier, you can make a purchase of products from them. To do this, go to the "Purchases" view and click the new purchase button. Select the supplier from the "Supplier name" field, enter the invoice's number and reception date. Clicking the "Add an item" button you can enter an item at a time.

Finally, you can start recording sales in the "Checkout" view.
